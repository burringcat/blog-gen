Use Openvpn Behind a Socks5 Proxy Add the socks-proxy directive to your openvpn configuration file and you are done. socks-proxy 127.0.0.1 1080 But if you are like me, running a socks5 proxy on localhost or some private IP address, the connection to the upstream of the proxy should go through your local gateway. The route directive solves this problem. Suppose your upstream ip is 123.123.123.123. route 123.123.123.123 255.255.255.255 net_gateway
